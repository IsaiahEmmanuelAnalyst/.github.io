01 · About

A bit about me

I just wrapped up my Postgraduate Diploma in Business Analytics at Lambton College, building on a BSc and 5+ years of hands-on data work spanning logistics, manufacturing, and product.

My specialty is the unglamorous middle: gathering scrappy business requirements, translating them into clean data models, then shipping the dashboards, paginated reports, and automations that turn raw operational data into something a stakeholder will actually open.

I care about analytics that gets used. A Power BI report nobody clicks is just decoration. I'm equally comfortable writing SQL, mapping a process workflow, or walking a non-technical user through what their numbers really mean.
